About the Company

A new generation of Ethiopian engineering excellence.

Founded on April 7, 2024, Echo Engineering PLC was built to meet the rising demand for skilled, vendor-certified telecom and electro-mechanical engineering in Ethiopia and East Africa. We combine deep field experience with disciplined operations to deliver projects to international standards.

◎ Our Vision

To become one of the leading telecom and electro-mechanical engineering companies in Ethiopia and East Africa by delivering innovative, reliable, and high-quality technical solutions.

▶ Our Mission

  • Provide professional telecom and electro-mechanical engineering services
  • Support telecom network expansion and infrastructure development
  • Maintain the highest standards of safety, quality, and customer satisfaction
  • Build long-term partnerships with local and international companies

Delivery Methodology

A disciplined, repeatable process for every project.

Our delivery model is built around vendor MOPs (Methods of Procedure), HSE compliance, and clear acceptance criteria — ensuring predictability for clients and accountability for our teams.

1

Survey & Planning

Site walk, technical assessment, BoM verification, MOP review.

2

Mobilization

Crew assignment, tool & PPE check, safety briefing, logistics.

3

Installation

Vendor-spec installation, cabling, grounding, power-up.

4

Integration & Test

Commissioning, performance verification, ATP documentation.

5

Handover & Support

Snag closure, as-built docs, warranty support, PM cycles.
